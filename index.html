About the 3D Art Gallery

Welcome to our virtual 3D Art Gallery. Here, you can navigate through a variety of wonderful artworks and learn about their history.

We believe in the power of art to inspire, provoke thought, and communicate across cultures and epochs. Our mission is to make art accessible to everyone, regardless of location or background.

Feel free to explore and engage with the artworks. The gallery is best experienced in full-screen mode. For optimal navigation, use the arrow keys or the W, A, S, and D keys. You can also use your mouse to change the viewing direction. Additional information about the artworks will appear on your screen as you approach them.

Controls

[Z/Q/S/D] or Arrows : Move around

Mouse: Look around

Space: Toggle pointer lock

M: Show Menu

Enter: Start exploration

Esc: Stop exploration

G: Start audio

P: Stop audio
